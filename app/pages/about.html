About

The Etna Old Time Association is a new-old-time duo rooted on the banks of Mink Brook in Etna, NH. We forage from a collection of traditional tunes and newer tunes and stew them in a broth of bluegrass and folk. We hope you enjoy the music!

Please get in touch to book us for your next farmer’s market, barn-raising, or wedding!
